Privacy Policy — Last Wire

Last updated: 2 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game Last Wire ("the app", package com.lastwire.game) handles information. The app is published by the Last Wire developer ("we", "us").

The short version

Last Wire does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The game is fully offline and has no accounts, no logins, no advertising, and no analytics or tracking.

Information we collect

None. We do not collect any personal or sensitive user data. We do not ask for your name, email, location, contacts, photos, or any identifiers.

Data stored on your device

The app saves your game progress (such as your current level, stars earned, in-game coins, and settings like sound and reduced-motion) locally on your device only, using the device's standard app storage. This information never leaves your device, is never sent to us or any third party, and is removed if you uninstall the app or clear its data.

Network access

The app does not require an internet connection and does not send data over the network during normal play.

Third-party services

The app does not include third-party advertising, analytics, or social SDKs that collect data. It is built with the open-source Capacitor framework; the bundled plugins (app lifecycle, haptics/vibration, local preferences storage, splash screen, status bar) operate entirely on-device and do not transmit data.

Children's privacy

The app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "last updated" date.
